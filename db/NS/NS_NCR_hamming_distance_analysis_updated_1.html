NS H1Nx Forward 1.4 primer NCR hamming distances

NS_H1_F <- read.csv("hamming_distance_results/NS_H1_NS_Forward_1.4_results_NCR.csv")

NS_H1_1 <- NS_H1_F[-c(2817),]

barplot(table(NS_H1_1$hamming_distance), main = "NS H1NX hamming distances, forward primer", xlab = "Hamming Distance", ylab = "Number of Sequences", ylim = c(0,3000))

NS Forward 1.4 primer: AGA GCA AAA GCA GGG TGAC AAA

Melting temperature: 57.4C

Hairpins (G < -1): 0

Self-dimers (G < -6): 0

Heterodimers (G < -6): 0

This primer is universal for the NS segment of all strains of Influenza A. The primer includes a 2 nucleotide long 5 tail (italized) that is not based on the sequences in order to increase the melting temperature and improve other factors of the primer; this tail was not included in the hamming distance calculations. The primer consists of the universal non-coding region (AGC AAA AGC AGG, bolded), and part of the non-coding region that is unique to the NS segment. Based on the alignment shade threshold and hamming distance calculations, 89% of NS H1Nx sequences will have a hamming distance of 0 with this primer. 2,815 H1N1 sequences and 1 H1N2 sequence were used for this alignment and calculations.
